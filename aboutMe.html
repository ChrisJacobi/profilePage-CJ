My full name is Christopher Ryan Jacobi and I have a passion for web development. But thats not all. Besides coding, I have an educational background in music theory and spent many years singing in choir as a tenor in high school and college. I graduated from Eastern High School with an advanced diploma after spending three years in an a capella group called "E major". We sang at many competitions spaning across the United States. I've been lucky enough to be able to perform with Teddy Abrams and the Louisville Orchestra twice as a choir member. What an experience! You see, my passion for music and creativity led me to web development. Much like writing a song, I type code to display a presentation of my imagination. Coding is a journey, and I am improving everyday. I enjoy solving problems and conquering challenges; there is nothing I can't learn.
